The World is a Puzzle

When I was 6, I had a world map jigsaw puzzle. That's where I first learned of "exotic" places like Borneo, the Amazon river, Lake Titicaca, and Mount Kilimanjaro.

Curiosity about the geography and cultures of the world has been the backdrop of my life ever since. It's taken me places I never thought I would go, and it's given me the opportunity to meet people from every continent (except Antarctica -- but I have met some awesome penguins).
